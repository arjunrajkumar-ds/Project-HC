Home Screen — Bodyweight Exercise Logging

Wireframes for Gayathri. Goal: open the panel, tap an exercise, log a set (+10 reps), and move on — with a clear view of remaining exercises by muscle group. Frequent, low-friction logging throughout the day.

Desktop / web panel +10 = one set of 10 reps Grouped by muscle group Low-fidelity — layout only, no visual design
1

Grouped List (accordion)

Muscle groups stacked as collapsible sections. Each group shows its progress; expand to see exercises with an inline +10. Best when the exercise list is long — everything scannable in one column, completed groups collapse away.

2

Card Grid Dashboard (filter chips)

Every exercise is a tile with a large tap target. Muscle-group chips across the top filter and show remaining count per group. Fastest for at-a-glance tapping when she already knows what she wants — big buttons, minimal reading.

3

Focus + Queue

One exercise takes centre stage with an oversized +10; a queue on the right shows what's next and what's left by group. Best for a guided "just tell me what to do" flow — she logs the highlighted exercise, then it advances to the next pending one.
